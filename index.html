UNTRANSLATABLE

three untranslatable words in cantonese. growing up in a immigrant household, my first language was cantonese. when i finally started learning english in school, i realized that some terms my family would use couldn’t be said in english: UNTRANSLATABLE. as i’ve grown older and now live away from home and my cantonese slowly slips away, i hold these untranslatable terms close to my heart.
